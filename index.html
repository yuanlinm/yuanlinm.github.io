About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in epidemiology, focusing on the impact of ambient air pollution on lung cancer in never smokers. I utilize both macOS and Linux for my research. Most of my work involves traditional association studies, including Cox regression, mediation, and interaction analyses. Additionally, I have experience with bioinformatics analyses, such as genome-wide association studies and Mendelian randomization. I am a self-motivated worker who enjoys conducting independent investigations. Email: boomers_clip.1e@icloud.com
